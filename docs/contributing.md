# ᜆᜄᜊᜌ᜔ᜊᜌ᜔ / TagaBaybay: Contributing

Tagabaybay is currently a private invite-only Filipino loanword
adaptation project written in Rust. Even if its private, it is under
the [Apache 2.0 license](/LICENSE) and will be made public soon.

## know your role

- **Rule Developer**: you develop the rules for the rewrite system.
- **Evaluator**: you evaluate the performance of the rules,
creating evaluation methodologies and coming up with the evaluation
metrics. You also help construct the gold standards and look for edge cases.
